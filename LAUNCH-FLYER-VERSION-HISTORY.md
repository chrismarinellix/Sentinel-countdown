## Version 2.0 - Enhanced Legal Protection
**Date:** 2025-11-05
**Status:** Current Version

### Changes Made:
1. **Prize Section Updates:**
   - Removed specific dollar amount (AUD $1,500-2,000) from prize description
   - Changed prize order to: "MacBook Air OR Windows Laptop OR Cash Equivalent"
   - Simplified to "Winner's choice" without value reference

2. **Terms & Conditions - Enhanced Legal Protection:**
   - Added "may/could" language throughout to protect Vysus against changes
   - Made all obligations non-absolute using conditional language
   - Added stock availability clauses throughout

3. **Specific T&C Updates:**
   - **Prize Options:** Added "subject to availability" clauses
   - **Payment Method:** Changed to "may be paid via payroll... or via other payment method at management's discretion"
   - **Payment Timing:** Changed to "May be paid" (conditional)
   - **Payslip Notation:** Added flexibility - "May appear on payslip... at management's discretion"
   - **Typical Timeframe:** Added "subject to administrative processing and stock availability for physical prizes"
   - **Tax Section:** Changed all definitive statements to "may" language
   - **Prize Delivery:** Added "at management's discretion or winner's choice where feasible"
   - **Delivery Timeframe:** Added "subject to stock availability, supply chain conditions, and shipping logistics"
   - **Future Quarters:** Added "may be offered... subject to trial results and management approval"
   - **Prize Value:** Added "may vary based on market conditions, exchange rates, and availability"

4. **Important Things to Understand:**
   - Added: "decisions may be influenced by changing business priorities"
   - Added: "rules, timelines, prizes, and processes may change at management's discretion"
   - Added: "we reserve the right to disqualify entries"
   - Added new clause: "Program may be modified or discontinued at any time without prior notice or obligation"

5. **Trial Program Notice:**
   - Added friendly explanation about quarterly prizes being a trial
   - Clarified that prize element may be removed pending trial results
   - Maintained positive tone while protecting Vysus

6. **Sentinel PROGRAM Scope:**
   - Extended to include: "Extends to training, learning, and all aspects of our business"

### Key Protection Features:
- All commitments now conditional using "may/could/might"
- Stock availability clauses protect against supply chain issues
- Management discretion explicitly stated throughout
- Tax obligations fully transferred to winner with conditional language
- Program modification/discontinuation rights reserved
- Payment method flexibility maintained
- No absolute promises or guarantees
